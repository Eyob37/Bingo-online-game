How to Play

  • Each player gets a 5×5 board with numbers 1-25
  • Players take turns selecting cells
  • All players mark the same number on their boards
  • Complete rows, columns, or diagonals to earn B-I-N-G-O letters
  • First player to spell "BINGO" wins!
